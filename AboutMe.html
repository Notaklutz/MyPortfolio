About Me


I am a Grade 10 student in the MaCS enriched program at William Lyon Mackenzie C.I. who is currently taking AP Computer Science.

 

I enjoy and am interested in reading, writing, science, programming, travelling, photography, and nature.

 

According to the Keirsey Temperament Sorter, I am a "Guardian". Like 45% of the population, I am dependable, helpful, and hard-working. Guardians such as myself are pragmatists who value justice, humility, gratitude, and authority. Finally, I take great pride in fulfilling my duties and responsibilities as well as honouring traditions and customs.

My personality type according to the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is ISTJ (introversion, sensing, thinking, and judgement). Like other ISTJs, I have a good sense of morality and responsibility, as well as punctuality and commitment. I tackle problems using a methodical approach and tend to stick with effective strategies rather than explore new ones. Integrity, duty, and pragmatism define me, enabling me to complete tasks with the utmost precision and efficiency.

 

Despite the many strengths of ISTJs, as with any personality type, there are multiple weaknesses as well. I can be stubbornly resistant to trying new strategies and can sometimes appear to be insensitive or robotic.

 

Overall, my personality type and temperament define me as someone who is trustworthy, reliable, and duty-bound. I will be practical in my problem-solving ventures and have a strong sense of morality in everything that I do. It is these strengths that make me a great student and worker.
