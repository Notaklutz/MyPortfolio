Advanced Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) Examples


Virtual School

Virtual school is an assignment that I did for my AP Computer Science class. The program simulates a virtual school, with students who have lockers, jackets, books, courses, teachers, etc. Its main function is to determine whether or not a student is able to enter a classroom. It implements many of the core OOP principles. Press the buttons below to scroll through the program's code.
